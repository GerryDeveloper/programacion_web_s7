  - in

- Relative Units
  there are 2 types 
  - relative to font-size 

  - em 
  - rem 

  - relative to the view-port (browser window)

  - vw
  - vh
  - vmin
  - vmax

- Percentage Units
  - percentage are mainly used for widths
  - relative to their parent (except height)
  - el porcentaje es relativo al compnente padre
